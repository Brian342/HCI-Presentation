Ecitizen

Hello welcome to the Ecitizen Design Project
in this Project we are tackling and handling the Passport Application System and
the Settings Systems on the Ecitizen Platform..

Passport System

ECitizen Passport Service is the official digital platform used by the Government of Kenya to facilitate the application, renewal and replacement of Kenyan passports. It is managed by the Directorate of Immigration Services and acts as a central hub to move the traditionally manual process into a more efficient, digital-first system..

Settings

ECitizen Settings Service (often referred to as Profile Management) is the core administrative component of the Kenyan eCitizen portal. It acts as the central hub where users manage their digital identity, security credentials, and personal information

ChatBot

an AI-powered digital assistant designed to help Kenyans and residents navigate the vast array of government services on the eCitizen portal..
